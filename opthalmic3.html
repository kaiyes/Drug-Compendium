Dexamethasone 0.1% and tobramycin 0.3%: Eye drop & ointment. Ind: Steroid responsive inflammatory ocular conditions where bacterial infections or risk of bacterial infections co-exist. C/I; S/E; Cautions: For tobramycin see above under the text of tobramycin eye preparation; for dexamethasone, see above under the text of steroid eye products. Paediatric use only by physicians experienced in ophthalmology. Dosage & admin: Eye drop: 1 or 2 drops instilled into the conjunctival sac(s) every 4 to 6 hours. During the initial 24 to 48 hurs, the dosage may be increased to 1-2 drops every two hours. Frequency should be decreased gradually as warranted by improvement in clinical signs. Care should be taken not to discontinue therapy prematurely. Ointment: apply a small amount into the conjunctival sac(s) up to 3 or 4 times daily. After applying ointment, look downward before closing the eye. Ointment may be used at bedtime in conjunction with suspension used during the day.
